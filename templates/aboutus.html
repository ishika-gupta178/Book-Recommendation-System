Just Read it!

A book recommendation system

Books play a very important role in every person's life by introducing them to a world of imagination, providing knowledge of the outside world, improving their reading, writing and speaking skills as well as boosting memory and intelligence, all of which are quite necessary for different aspects of life.

There exists many potential readers, but due to the abundance of information present on the internet, many of these people find it extremely hard to search for books that they might like and might inculcate in them a habit of reading, which is always encouraged. This could result in a huge loss as a lack of readings results in poor language skills, cultural ignorance, and a fear of books. Furthermore, many people ask for book recommendations from their friends and families who might not always suggest the right book as they do not have knowledge about the numerous books that are available.

How convenient if all this process was taken care of automatically and recommended the book efficiently? A recommendation system is the answer to this question.

Consequently, the main aim of our mini project is to build a book recommendation system. It can be used by anyone, and does not require any login making it much more accessible and easy to use. The user needs to just enter the title of the book that they have read and liked before, and based on the genre and rating given to the book, 10 books will be recommended to the user that are the most similar to the book. that they have entered.

Happy reading!
